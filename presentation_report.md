# Security Agency Management Software — Product Overview

## Executive Summary
A comprehensive, end-to-end ERP (Enterprise Resource Planning) software custom-built for Security Agencies. The platform digitizes and automates every aspect of agency operations—from tracking guard attendance and generating client invoices, to processing payroll, managing vendor expenses, and providing deep financial analytics.

This system eliminates manual paperwork, reduces billing errors, ensures statutory compliance (GST, TDS, PF, ESI), and provides business owners with real-time visibility into their profitability.

---

## Core Modules & Features

### 1. 📊 Executive Dashboard
The command center for agency owners and managers.
- **Real-Time KPIs**: View total active clients, active guards, monthly revenue, and pending collections at a glance.
- **Financial Health**: Visual charts tracking revenue vs. expenses over time.
- **Smart Alerts Centre**: Proactive notifications for:
  - Contracts expiring within 60 days
  - Overdue client invoices
  - Pending payroll disbursements

### 2. 🏢 Client Management
Complete lifecycle management for security contracts.
- **Client Profiles**: Store contract details, billing addresses, GST numbers, and primary contacts.
- **Contract Tracking**: Track contract start and end dates, with automated renewal reminders.
- **Billing Configuration**: Set fixed monthly rates per client site.
- **Site Assignment**: Link guards directly to client sites for accurate cost allocation.

### 3. 👥 Employee & Attendance Management
Streamline workforce tracking and deployment.
- **Guard Profiles**: Maintain comprehensive records including Aadhaar, UAN (PF), ESIC numbers, and bank details.
- **Dynamic Salary Structures**: Assign customizable salary structures (Base, DA, HRA) with automated statutory deductions.
- **Site Allocation**: Deploy guards to specific client locations.
- **Attendance Tracking**: 
  - Daily or monthly attendance logging
  - Automatic calculation of days worked vs. days in month

### 4. 📄 Invoicing & Billing
Automated, compliance-ready financial billing.
- **One-Click Invoicing**: Generate invoices based on client monthly rates with a single click.
- **Tax Compliance (GST)**: Automatic calculation of CGST, SGST, and IGST based on location.
- **Discount & RCM**: Support for custom discounts and Reverse Charge Mechanism (RCM).
- **Payment Tracking**: Record partial or full payments against invoices.
- **TDS Handling**: Automatically track TDS deducted by clients during payment.
- **PDF Generation**: Download professional, print-ready PDF invoices.

### 5. 💰 Payroll Processing
Complex security payroll made simple and error-free.
- **Automated Calculation**: Calculates gross and net salary based on days worked.
- **Statutory Deductions**: Automatically computes PF (12%) and ESI (0.75%) based on the assigned salary structure.
- **Bulk Processing**: Generate payslips for the entire workforce simultaneously.
- **Payslip Generation**: Download professional PDF payslips for guards.
- **Disbursement Tracking**: Mark salaries as paid and track pending disbursements.

### 6. 🧾 Expense & Vendor Management
Track where every rupee goes.
- **Vendor Profiles**: Manage uniform suppliers, equipment providers, and office vendors.
- **Expense Logging**: Record operational expenses categorized by type (Uniforms, Equipment, Office, etc.).
- **Approval Workflow**: Expenses can be drafted, approved, and then paid.
- **Vendor Ledger**: Track total billed vs. total paid for every vendor to manage payables.

### 7. 📘 Financial Ledgers
Transparent accounting for both receivables and payables.
- **Client Ledger**: Track every invoice, payment, and TDS deduction for a specific client to see exactly what is owed.
- **Employee Ledger**: View a complete history of salaries generated and payments made to a specific guard.

### 8. 📈 Advanced Reporting & Analytics
Data-driven insights for business growth.
- **Revenue Trends**: Monthly revenue breakdowns by client.
- **Advanced Metrics**: 
  - **DSO (Days Sales Outstanding)**: Track how fast clients pay.
  - **Client Profitability**: See exactly how much profit each client generates (Revenue minus assigned Guard Payroll).
  - **Business Health Score**: A proprietary 0-100 score analyzing margins, collections, and labor ratios.
- **Profit & Loss Account**: 
  - A formal, CA-ready P&L statement.
  - Breaks down Income, Cost of Services (Payroll), and Operating Expenses.
  - Shows Gross Margin and Net Margin.
  - Includes Year-over-Year comparison and Monthly trend charts.

### 9. 🗄️ Statement Archive (Compliance Hub)
A permanent, unalterable digital filing cabinet.
- **Auto-Archiving**: Every time a critical financial document is created or paid (Invoice, Payslip, Expense), a snapshot is permanently saved.
- **5 Domains**: Seamlessly tracks Invoices, GST Entries, TDS Certificates, Vendor Statements, and Payroll.
- **Audit-Ready**: Quickly search, filter by date, and export records for auditors or tax consultants.

### 10. ⚙️ System Administration
Enterprise-grade control and security.
- **Role-Based Access Control (RBAC)**: Distinct permissions for Admin, Manager, and Accountant roles.
- **Settings Dashboard**: Configure agency details, default tax rates, and upload a dynamic Agency Logo for all PDFs.
- **Developer Console**: A hidden, passcode-protected (`M$sterC0de`) dashboard that tracks system health and logs all backend errors in real-time, ensuring maximum uptime.

## Key Benefits for the Agency

1. **Zero Revenue Leakage**: Automated billing ensures no client is under-billed and overdue payments trigger alerts.
2. **100% Statutory Compliance**: PF, ESI, GST, and TDS are calculated mathematically, eliminating human error and compliance penalties.
3. **Reduced Admin Overhead**: What used to take 5 days of manual ledger entry at month-end now takes 5 minutes.
4. **Actionable Intelligence**: The owner instantly knows which client contracts are highly profitable and which are losing money.
5. **Professional Image**: System-generated PDFs for invoices and payslips project a highly professional corporate image to clients and staff.
