1. Balance Sheet New in v1.2.2

02

A real-time Balance Sheet — the most important financial document any business can produce. It shows a complete snapshot of your agency's financial position at any given moment.

Crucial Business Value

The Balance Sheet is the document that banks require when you apply for a business loan. It is also the primary document your Chartered Accountant needs for annual tax filing. Generate it in one click instead of paying your CA to compile it manually.

Liabilities Side

  • Capital & Reserves: Owner's equity and retained profits
  • Current Liabilities: Pending salaries
  • Accounts Payable: Outstanding vendor payments

Assets Side

  • Fixed Assets: Equipment, vehicles, computers
  • Accounts Receivable: Unpaid client invoices
  • Bank Balances: Real-time liquid cash

2. Universal Vouchers New in v1.2.2

03

A professional, double-entry accounting system with 8 specialized voucher types — the backbone of accurate financial recording in any business.

The 8 Voucher Types

Voucher Type When To Use It (Real-World Example)
Cash Payment Paying ₹1,500 to a stationery shop for office supplies
Bank Receipt Client transfers ₹55,000 invoice payment to your HDFC account
Contra Depositing ₹10,000 physical cash into your bank account
Journal Entry Recording ₹2,000 depreciation on office computers
Credit Note Giving ₹3,000 discount for a service interruption

Strict Approval Workflow: Every voucher goes through a secure lifecycle: Draft → Pending Approval → Posted. Once posted, it becomes a permanent immutable record in your ledger.

3. Bank Reconciliation New in v1.2.2

04

A bank reconciliation tool that ensures your software's records match your actual bank statement — the ultimate accuracy check for your finances.

The Proof of Truth

When your Balance Sheet says you have ₹5,00,000 in the bank, Bank Reconciliation verifies that this number matches reality by checking against your HDFC or SBI bank statement. Without it, errors go undetected.

Reconciliation Workflow

  • Download your monthly bank statement PDF from HDFC/SBI
  • Select the corresponding bank account in the software
  • Compare each voucher against the bank statement line-by-line
  • Reconcile: Check the box next to matching transactions to permanently verify them

Common Discrepancies Handled Automatically

Easily identify cheques that haven't cleared yet, hidden bank maintenance charges, or simple data entry typos.

5. Security & Architecture

06

The software implements enterprise-grade security suitable for handling sensitive employee and financial data.

100% Offline Architecture

Your agency's data is your most valuable asset. SecurManage stores everything locally on your PC in a secure SQLite database. It works perfectly without an internet connection, ensuring complete data privacy and zero cloud subscription fees.

Granular Team Access Control

Ensure each team member only sees what they need:

  • Office Manager: Manage Attendance, Employees, Payroll. (Cannot see profits)
  • Accountant: Manage Invoices, Vouchers, Bank Reconciliation, Balance Sheet.
  • Site Supervisor: Only mark attendance.

Hidden Developer Console

A Matrix-style console silently captures every application error in the background. It provides stack traces and debugging information without ever interrupting the user experience for your staff.

Seamless Auto-Updates

SecurManage checks for updates automatically and downloads them in the background. When you close the app, it updates instantly with zero downtime. Your database is completely isolated and always safe during updates.
